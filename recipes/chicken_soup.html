Chicken Soup

Back to Home page

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 8 cups water
  • 2 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup egg noodles
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:

  • In a large pot, add the chicken pieces and water. Bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is cooked through.
  • Add carrots, celery, onion, garlic, thyme, bay leaf, salt, and pepper. Simmer for another 15 minutes.
  • Stir in egg noodles and cook for 5 more minutes until noodles are tender.
  • Remove bay leaf and stir in parsley. Serve hot.
