Nigerian Jollof Rice

Back to Home page

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound cooked beef, sliced

Instructions:

  • Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Set aside.
  • In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and red bell pepper, and saut√© until softened.
  • Add the diced tomatoes and tomato paste to the pot. Cook for about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Add the chicken broth, thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
  • Add the rinsed rice to the pot. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  • Fluff the rice with a fork and stir in the cooked beef slices. Serve hot.
