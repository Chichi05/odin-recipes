Vegetable Omelette

Back to Home page

Ingredients:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:

  • In a bowl, whisk together eggs and milk. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and spinach. Sauté until vegetables are tender.
  • Pour the egg mixture over the sautéed vegetables in the skillet. Cook until the edges start to set.
  • Carefully flip the omelette and cook for another 2-3 minutes until fully cooked.
  • Slide the omelette onto a plate, fold it in half, and serve hot.
